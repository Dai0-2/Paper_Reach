# Scoring

- Topic relevance: `0-3`
- Method match: `0-3`
- Dataset match: `0-2`
- Supervision or annotation match: `0-2`
- Evidence confidence: `0-2`

Typical guidance:

- `10-12`: strong candidate, but still check hard constraints
- `7-9`: plausible but may remain ambiguous
- `0-6`: weak match or likely reject
